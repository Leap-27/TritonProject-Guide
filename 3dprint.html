Triton Project 3D Printing Guide

Overview

The Triton's design consists of 15 custom designed 3d printed parts. If you plan to use your own 3d printer you'll need to 3D print roughly 20+ hours. There's nothing expensive or major requiring a machine shop, but a few tools are expected.
